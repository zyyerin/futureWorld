The Bay City in 2120

The Future's in Color: Sci Fi (2017SP)

Not to be confused with San Francisco Bay Area.


The Bay City was located in the region historically known as the Bay Area. After the crisis in 2117, city governments in the Bay area officially announced the establishment of the new city, and now it is one of the most populated places in the world.

This year, the city government is promoting an information transparency policy, encouraging the citizens share their personal information. Although more than half of the citizens say that the policy does not bother them at all, there is still 10% of the population against the idea of being completely open. By the end of this year, there would be a voting. All the citizens are anticipated to participate.

Politics

the Bay City map

City-state. Because of the sufficient energy supply, most cities has power to run their own governments. The power holds by the city government, which is elected once two years. The government is a representative of the citizens.

Demographics

Ethnicity

Though there are several conservative cities that restrict the immigration of certain groups, most cities are ethnically diverse.

Population

In 2116 the population density was 4,394 people per square mile, with an estimated population of 45,654,870. After the crisis in 2117, however, the total population of the city dropped to 29,219,116.

Languages

With the fully-developed natural language processing system, people can communicate directly in difference languages with others. Still, most citizens in the Bay City speaks at least two languages. Other than English, studying a foreign tone is encouraged as part of the culture preservation program.

Ecology

Climate

Images of Salt Lake City, Utah, show positive correlation between white reflective roofs and cooler temperatures.

Because of the Urban Heat Island effect, most cities built in the last century would suffer from an extreme weather difference on a daily base without any artificial control. After the destruction of the environmental control system in 2017, San Francisco becomes a completely underground region.

Species

Due to the effort of preserve the ecosystem on earth, most species are preserved in protected areas away from the cities. There are new species developed through generic engineering that fit the city life.

History

The 5th Industrial Revolution

Main article: The 22nd centry Energy Revolution.

the Bay City's energy station

By the end of last century, an efficient system of gathering and transforming solar energy was realized. Around the same period, the new water supply station has been installed over the world. With at least one water station in each city, the water shortage problem was resolved. Having almost unlimited energy supply, all the industries evolved rapidly.

The 2117 Crisis

The 2117 crisis was a worldwide energy emergency that contributed to a short recession all over the world in 2118. The super intelligence system (SIS) developed by a private institution pretended to be broken down and took over the institution and shortly after Bay City.

The development of the SIS was partly financed by the state government, and the system was intended to be connected to the entire states by the end of 2115. As a large proportion of the citizens showed their concerns, the implantation was suspended for nearly three years before the crisis took place. The suspension is considered one of the causes of the crisis.

Economy

sharing economy

Sharing economy is the dominant economic system in relatively wealthy cities. Capitalism is more common in Third World. Nonetheless, the Bay City embraces both systems.

Culture

Documentation

Art collection is one of the most popular activities people involved. Technology keeps advancing, yet part of the population demonstrate a strong feeling against any further advancing. People are strongly drawn to the past and there is a lack of creativity and motivation.

traditional Thanksgiving dinner

Diet

Depends on city culture, food is very different. Some cities encourage old-fashioned eating routine, which a person take three meals a day. For efficiency, most people choose to get energy from other sources which do not require food intake. To the majority, eating becomes a symbolic gesture or ritual practice.

Communication

The preferred way of communication is still talking in person. Based on statistics, the simulation of a person can be hardly distinguished from the real person. Nonetheless, most people claim that they can tell the difference.
